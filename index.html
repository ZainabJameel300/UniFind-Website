UniFind – Smart Lost & Found for Students

Revolutionizing how UOB students report and recover lost items with AI-powered matching and real-time notifications.

About the App

UniFind simplifies lost and found processes with intelligent features designed for campus life.

Report Items

Quickly report lost or found items with photos, descriptions, and location details.

AI Matching

Our CLIP-based AI model analyzes images and descriptions to find potential matches.

Secure Chat

Communicate safely with other users to verify ownership and arrange pickup.

Real-Time Alerts

Get instant notifications when a potential match is found for your items.

How It Works

Recover lost items in just a few simple steps with UniFind's intelligent platform.

1

Report

Report a lost or found item by adding details, descriptions, photos, and location. Our AI analyzes the information to find possible matches.

2

Match

Our AI compares your item with all reported items, considering both text and visual similarities.

3

Notify

When a potential match is found, matched posts owners receives real-time notifications with the match details.

4

Recover

Use the in-app chat to verify ownership and arrange pickup. Mark items as claimed when recovered.

Why UniFind?

Designed specifically for UOB university campuses to address common student challenges.

Faster Recovery

Cut recovery time from days to hours with AI-powered matching and instant notifications.

Stress-Free Experience

No more wandering around campus or checking multiple bulletin boards. Everything is in one app.

Safe Communication

In-app chat keeps personal contact information private until you're ready to share it.

AI-Powered Accuracy

Our CLIP-based model understands both images and text descriptions for better matching accuracy.
